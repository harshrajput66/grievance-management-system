Platform Features

Everything You Need

A complete platform to raise, track, and resolve civic complaints — built for citizens and administrators alike.

01

Easy Complaint Submission

Submit complaints with title, description, category, and priority. Attach image or PDF proof. A unique Complaint ID is auto-generated on submission.

02

Real-Time Status Updates

Live notifications via Socket.IO — the moment your complaint status changes, you are notified instantly without refreshing the page.

03

Complete Complaint Timeline

Every action — submission, admin review, status updates, and remarks — is logged in a chronological timeline visible to the citizen.

04

Admin Control Dashboard

Administrators can search, filter, review uploaded proof, add remarks, update status, and resolve or reject cases through a structured interface.

05

Secure & Authenticated

JWT-based authentication and bcrypt password hashing. Role-based access separates citizen and administrator permissions completely.

06

Reopen & Appeal

If unsatisfied with a resolution, citizens may reopen a complaint with a stated reason, triggering a fresh administrative review cycle.

Process

How It Works

A simple four-step process to get your complaint registered and resolved.

1
Register & Sign In

Create a citizen account with your name, email, and contact details. Existing users may sign in directly to their dashboard.

2
Submit Complaint

Fill in the complaint form, select a category and priority, and optionally attach photographic or documentary evidence.

3
Track Progress

Monitor real-time status changes and admin remarks through your personal dashboard. Receive live notifications on every update.

4
Resolution or Appeal

Once resolved, the case closes. If unsatisfied, reopen it with a reason for a fresh administrative review.
